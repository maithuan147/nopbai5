APPLICATION

Administering the Application Process

Our goal is to make the process simple for our clients - whether you are in middle school, secondary school, or high school. We understand that transitioning from one education system to another can be challenging, even more so when you're entering a totally new environment. Our consultant will guide you through the process and support you with administering the entire application from start to finish. This way, you can focus on what matters - your new chapter in education.

HOMESTAY

Homestay/Guardian

Arranging homestay and finding a suitable family for your child who is pursuing an education overseas can be a challenge, especially if your child is under the age of 18. We personally interview and select each guardian, ensuring that each possess years of guardianship experience with overseas students. Upon communicating your needs and desires with us, we will recommend suitable guardians for your selection. Each individual is unique and our aim is to match the most suitable guardian to the child, ensuring the most relevant living environment is arranged for your child.

INTERNSHIP

Internship Program

Experience a global work experience.

The aim for this program is for the student to experience and effectively inducts them into the nature, structure and organisation of a company and their future work, roles and responsibilities. From this they can further develop their vision and build international relationships and network.

Throughout the internship, they are expected to:

  • Develop awareness, understanding and capacity in the specific roles and responsibilities in their subject and as far as possible, to effectively participate in these roles and responsibilities
  • Understand through an intensive experience the nature of Asian/ international business as workplaces and their associated values, routines and cultures
  • Further develop and refine their skills and the professional capacity in their subject specialisation
  • Demonstrate an understanding of professional and ethical practice
  • Develop an awareness of other people in the company and community

Committed to our chosen trade, we provide personalized attention and exceptional service. Every client is unique and deserves only the best.
